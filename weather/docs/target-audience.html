Target Audience

This is the target audience of Allweather.com:

  • Who: Climatologist, Storm Chasers, Environmental Scientists, and many others who wants to know the weather to plan according to it.
  • Age: 12-100
  • Technology: People access the weather through Computers, Alexa Devices, Cell Phones, Tablets, Smart Watches, Television, and Radio.
    They use this technology devices to get the most accurate and live weather forecast they can get.
  • Income: Fortunately, these days, it is extremely easy to know the forecast because almost everyone has a phone where they have access to weather information.
    People with low income can get the forecast through radio and television, and rich people, can access it from any other device they may have.
  • Motivation: What moves you is the ability to provide the most accurate and accesible forecast for everyone who want to know about it, and provide depper forcast and suggestions according the weather conditions.
